A cheerful and warm-hearted individual, Yearza Aqila Maitsa is someone who radiates positivity and enjoys the simple things in life. Born on February 2, 2011, and studying at SMP Vidya Dahana Patra in Bontang, East Kalimantan, she has a deep love for reading novels, exploring philosophy and psychology, and aspires to become a psychologist or dentist to help others.

Affectionate and caring, Yearza is also a true cat lover, finding comfort and peace in the company of her furry companions. Her favorite colors, including pink, light blue, white, cream, and brown, reflect her calm and nurturing nature. She enjoys indulging in matcha and treating herself to the occasional Kinder Joy.

As an ENFP, Yearza is spontaneous, creative, and full of energy. She values words of affirmation and acts of service, showing her deep belief in kindness and authentic connections. Yearza enjoys listening to music by artists like HIVI!, NIKI, Taylor Swift, and Nadin Amizah, and admires Jeong Jaehyun for his charm and talent.

In 2024, Yearza was proud to be chosen as Duta Siswa Berbakat Putri Kota Bontang, a title that celebrates her as a promising young ambassador of her city.
